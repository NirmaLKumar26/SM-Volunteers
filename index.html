About Service Motto Volunteers

We started with a few but now we are many, not only in numbers but also in talent and creativity. Service Motto volunteers started their service from October 5 2021 and continue their offer of service till now. We started our forum by inking a MOU with Bhumi, following it we celebrated the volunteer festival DAAN UTSAV. Rather than providing only service, we provide education, women health, women empowerment and self defence. We also provide internships for students for their self betterment. Volunteering is a service which is done to fix the dents of society. SM volunteers main motto is to foster a society. We have received honourable awards like Leelavati under women and adolescent health for the year 2021 and the Bhumi award for Best Volunteering Engagement for the year 2022.
