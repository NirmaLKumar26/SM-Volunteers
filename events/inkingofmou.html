Inking Of MOU

Volunteerism is one of the most vital delivery mechanisms for social, environmental, and economic transformation, ensuring a lasting impact with its ability to change people's mindsets, attitudes, and behaviors. K.S.Rangasamy College of Technology Volunteering team has planned for grand virtual Inauguration of the new volunteering forum by Inking MoU with Bhumi NGO as its first calendar event on 05/10/2021 from 10.30 A.M to 11.30 A.M. The name list of the second and third year registered students for volunteers was circulated to respective HoDs for necessary permission with respect to participation in Inauguration of the forum. All Deans, Directors, HODs, Faculty members, and all-volunteer students are invited to attend the inaugural via Microsoft Teams. Bhumi is India’s second-largest NGO which provides service to all the needy people. By signing in with bhumi we started many projects and we hope to continue them in the future. To serve the needy is a human right.
